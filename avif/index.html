Free AVIF Image Converter — Convert PNG, JPG, WebP to AVIF Online

Convert your images to the modern AVIF format directly in your browser. AVIF (AV1 Image File Format) offers up to 50% better compression than JPEG and 20% better than WebP — same visual quality at significantly smaller file sizes. Perfect for web developers optimizing page load speed. All conversion happens client-side — your images never leave your device.

How it works

  1. Drag and drop images (PNG, JPG, WebP) or click to browse — up to 20 files, max 50MB each
  2. Set quality using presets (Low 40, Medium 65, High 80, Lossless 100) or the slider
  3. Click Convert — watch the progress bar as each file is processed
  4. Download individual AVIF files or all at once as a ZIP archive

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AVIF format and why should I use it?

AVIF is a next-generation image format based on the AV1 video codec. It offers significantly better compression than JPEG, PNG, and even WebP. A typical JPEG image can be reduced by 50-70% when converted to AVIF with no visible quality loss. This means faster page loads and lower bandwidth costs.

How do I convert PNG to AVIF?

Simply drag your PNG files into the converter, set your desired quality (65 is recommended for web), and click Convert. The tool processes everything in your browser using WebAssembly — no upload needed. Download the resulting .avif files individually or as a ZIP.

Is AVIF better than WebP?

Yes, in most cases. AVIF typically achieves 20-30% smaller file sizes than WebP at the same visual quality. AVIF also supports wider color gamut, HDR, and better transparency handling. Browser support has reached over 95% in 2026.
