Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Summary

FormattedAI is a set of tools that run 100% client-side. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data. Your files and data never leave your device.

What data do we collect?

None. We have no backend server, don't log visits, and don't use cookies or analytics tools.

localStorage

The only data saved on your device are user preferences in browser localStorage:

  • Selected language (Polish/English)
  • Selected theme (light/dark)
  • Tool form data (e.g. SEO Tag Generator) — for autofill convenience

You can delete this data at any time by clearing your browser localStorage or clicking the "Clear" button in any tool.

Your files

Files you upload to tools (e.g. images in AVIF Converter) are processed exclusively in your browser. They are never sent to any server.

External resources

The site loads fonts from Google Fonts and (in AVIF Converter) a WASM module from esm.sh. These services may log HTTP requests.

Contact

Privacy questions? Contact us via GitHub Issues.
