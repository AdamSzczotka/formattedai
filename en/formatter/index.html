Markdown Formatter — ChatGPT to Google Docs and Word

Markdown Formatter lets you paste text generated by ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini and copy it to Google Docs or Microsoft Word with formatting preserved. Headers, bold, lists, code, tables — everything kept.

How it works

Paste Markdown on the left, the tool renders the preview on the right. Click "Copy formatted" and paste into Docs/Word — formatting preserved.

Is my text uploaded?

No. Everything runs in the browser (Marked + DOMPurify). Text never leaves your device.

Export formats

HTML (open in browser), DOCX (Microsoft Word), or Markdown (.md).
