FormattedAI — tools that don't see your data.

Free tools for web developers and content creators. Everything runs in your browser — zero uploads, full privacy.

Mission

FormattedAI was born from a simple need — webdev tools should be free, fast, and safe. Every tool runs 100% client-side. Your files, text, and data never leave your device.

We don't collect data, we don't require an account, we don't show ads. Just open the page and use it.

Privacy

Every FormattedAI tool runs exclusively in your browser. We have no backend server, we don't store files, we don't log activity. The only thing we save is your preferences (language, theme) in your browser's localStorage — on your device.

Frequently asked questions

What is FormattedAI?
FormattedAI is a collection of free online tools for web developers and content creators. It includes Markdown Formatter, AVIF Converter, HEIC Converter, OCR, JS Minifier, CSS Minifier, SEO & GEO Tag Generator, PDF Tools, HTML to PDF and Email Signature Builder. All tools run 100% in your browser.
Is my data safe?
Yes. All tools run entirely in your browser. Your files and data never leave your device — nothing is sent to any server.
How much does it cost?
FormattedAI is completely free. No accounts, no subscriptions, no hidden fees. The project is open-source under the MIT license.
What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?
GEO is a new standard for optimizing content for AI search engines — ChatGPT Search, Perplexity, Google AI Overview. It uses structured data (Schema.org), llms.txt files, and AI crawler configuration in robots.txt.
