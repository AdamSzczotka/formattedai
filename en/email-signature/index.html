Email Signature Builder - graphical generator for Outlook, Gmail, Thunderbird

    
FormattedAI Email Signature Builder lets you graphically design a professional email signature that renders identically across every popular mail client. Tables, inline CSS, MSO conditional comments and VML fallbacks for gradient buttons - all handled automatically.

    
How does it work?

    
Click a block in the left palette (text, image, button, divider, spacer), edit properties on the right (colors, font size, padding, link), click "Copy HTML" and paste into your signature settings in Gmail, Outlook or Thunderbird.

    
Will my signature work in Outlook?

    
Yes. The generator produces HTML compatible with Outlook desktop (Word rendering engine), Outlook 365 web, Gmail and Thunderbird. Gradient buttons include VML fallbacks for Outlook, which doesn't support CSS gradients on links.

    
Is my data uploaded?

    
No. Everything runs in your browser. Your signature is saved in your browser's localStorage - never sent to a server.
