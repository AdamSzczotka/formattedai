Free Online PDF Tools — Merge, Split, Compress PDF in Your Browser

Edit PDF files directly in your browser. Merge multiple PDF documents into one, split PDF into selected pages, compress PDF files and convert images to PDF. Everything runs 100% locally — your files are never sent to any server. The perfect tool for anyone who needs quick PDF editing without installing software.

How it works

  1. Choose a tab: Merge PDF, Split PDF, Compress or IMG → PDF
  2. Drag files onto the page or click Browse files
  3. Set options (order, pages to keep, compression level)
  4. Click the action button and download the resulting PDF file

Features

  • Merge multiple PDF files into one document with order preview
  • Split PDF — select pages to keep or download each one separately as ZIP
  • PDF compression — 3 presets (Light, Balanced, Maximum) + quality slider
  • Convert images (JPG, PNG, WebP, AVIF) to PDF
  • Page thumbnail previews and drag-and-drop ordering
  • Metadata stripping for better privacy
  • Works in all modern browsers
  • 100% client-side — your files never leave your device

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my files safe?

Yes. All files are processed entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server. We have no access to your documents — all logic runs locally on your device.

How to merge PDF files?

Drag PDF files onto the page, arrange order by dragging thumbnails, click Merge PDFs. Download the merged document with a single click.

How to extract pages from PDF?

Upload a PDF file, select the pages you want to keep on the thumbnail preview, click Split PDF. You can also download each page as a separate file in a ZIP archive.
