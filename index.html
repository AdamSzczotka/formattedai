FormattedAI - Free Markdown to Google Docs & Word Converter

FormattedAI is a free online tool that converts markdown text from ChatGPT, Claude, and other AI assistants into perfectly formatted rich text. Simply paste your AI-generated markdown, preview the formatting, and copy it to Google Docs or Microsoft Word with one click. No sign-up required.

Features

  • Convert ChatGPT markdown to Google Docs format
  • Convert AI text to Microsoft Word format
  • Preserve headings, tables, lists, bold, italic formatting
  • One-click copy with Ctrl+Shift+C shortcut
  • Free, no registration, works in browser
  • Available in Polish and English
